ABOUT US

The journey of M/s SHRIRAM ENTERPRISES started with a dream of Mr Manoj Gupta (Founder) to build a multidisciplinary engineering service firm in the field of industrial projects which is highly professional, performance driven and customer centric in its approach. The dream came true on 05 March 2015 when he finally founded the firm in Bhilai, Chhattisgarh- A region famous for its dense industrial activities. Our approach is customer centric i.e. identifying the project priorities of client and building the project execution plan accordingly. We deliver the plant within the scheduled timeline and to make that happen we plan our everyday activities, throughout the organisation, to achieve the goal. All our activities are centred towards the highest level of customer satisfaction.

M/s SHRIRAM ENTERPRISES (a MSME registered firm) having their Head Office at Bhilai and satellite offices at Rourkela, Odisha and Jagdalpur, Chhattisgarh specifically dealing in Industrial Turnkey Packages in EPC (Engineering Procurement and Construction) and standalone erection packages in Civil, Electrical, Pipelines, Mechanical, Electrical and C&I. We also cater to Operation & Maintenance Packages in Power Sector. With 1000+ young workforce we are serving our esteemed clients PAN India.
